Structures, Sorting and Such

Welcome to Structures, Sorting and Such! This is a website that I built to reinforce my understanding of various data structures, sorting algorithms and other miscellaneous algorithms. Data structures and algorithms are two critical concepts in the world of computer science. My hope is that this website will serve as a "quick reference guide" to not only myself but also others who may need a quick refresher on these topics.

I aimed to include some of the most popular and well known data structures and algorithms in this website. I feel like the collection that I discuss in this website encompasses the common data structures and algorithms that are brought up in schoolwork, job interviews and every day programming. Of course there are still many other "lesser known" structures and alorithms that I have not included in this website that are still important to know in the world of computer science.

Use the naviation bar at the top to select either Data Structures, Sorting Algorithms or Misc. Algorithms. The first page/link in the dropdown menu for each of these three categories gives a general overview of the given category. The preceding pages/links in the drop down menu will each cover a unique topic for the given category (Ex. the "Bubble Sort" link of the "Sorting Algorithms" dropdown menu will cover the bubble sort algorithm).

Click on the "About Me" link in the right side of the naviation bar to learn about who I am and how to contact me.

Thank you for visiting this website! Good luck and happy coding!
